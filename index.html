About Me

I'm currently an on-contract web developer for the National Association of the Deaf (NAD). How I got here is a different story.


While originally studying electronics, robotics, and networking, I found myself pulled to digital problems. I initially began work on applications primarily using the .NET Framework and Unity. While I began expanding my knowledge to the web side of the .NET framework I began working with the NAD.


At the NAD I've worked with Wordpress, CiviCRM, Linux, Powershell, PHP, and SQL. I've have both enjoyed and been massively proud of what we've accomplished together. This led me to strengthening my skills on the web side of things. My more recent work is a reflection of this. Including this very page you're reading this on.
